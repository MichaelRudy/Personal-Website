About Me

In recent years, I've developed a strong passion for self-directed learning in the field of computer science, driven by hands-on experience. After enrolling in several courses in a post-graduate computer science program at the University of Pennsylvania, I made a deliberate shift in my focus towards technology stacks that genuinely pique my interest. Currently, my primary goal is to blend my enthusiasm for technology with various hobbies I thoroughly enjoy. During the past year, I embarked on an exciting journey by initiating the development of an iOS application called 'Birdify.' This application is designed to gamify the score tracking experience for golfers, reflecting my commitment to creative and innovative projects. I envision this website as a platform to showcase not only my projects but also my diverse range of interests, all while providing insights into the processes and challenges encountered along the way.
